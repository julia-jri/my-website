Art & (Scientific) Illustrations

Hi, I'm Julia!
I enjoy art and like to create fun designs using different media. Recently, I have been experimenting with ink and linoleum prints. As a scientist, I appreciate effective and attention-grabbing scientific illustrations. In general, I like to focus on the beautiful things in life and bring the hidden, interesting details in to focus.

Linoleum

Linocut is a relief printing method using carved linoleum blocks.
The relief is colored and printed on white paper. The print itself is 10.5 x 15 cm².
